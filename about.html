Introduction

Transform Your Home
with Stylish Interiors

Established in 1990 as Ace Décor, we transitioned into real estate development in 1999 as Archit Developers. In 2001, we founded Impressions, specializing in residential projects. With over 17 years of experience, 500+ satisfied customers, and a portfolio exceeding 5 lakh sq. ft., we are a leading developer in Dombivli, creating lasting impressions through strategically located and thoughtfully designed homes.
